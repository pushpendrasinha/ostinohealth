  2. Senior executive onboarding

    3. Senior executive onboarding programs need to be tailored individually to the needs of an individual and the organization.

We took time out to figure key reasons for new executive failure through interactions, interviews and published literature. The outcome was that new executives generally fail due to five key reasons:

  • Cultural fitment.
  • Teamwork with staff and peers.
  • Unclear expectations about performance.
  • Political savviness.
  • Lack of a strategic and formal assimilation process.

Additional insight garnered in an Indian context was that some organizations do not emphasize onboarding of new executives as they wish to avoid offending existing professionals, who may perceive such program as hint of capability gaps. Research indicates that onboarding programs may be more critical as they face much higher performance expectations with a greater impact on the overall performance of the organization.

Recruitment and onboarding of new senior leaders – be it through internal promotion or external selection – should not be taken lightly. Proper planning with execution is the key.
