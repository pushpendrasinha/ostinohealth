Surajit Banerjee

Surajit Banerjee, a Chemical Engineer and an MBA by qualification, is the Founder and CEO of Ostino Enterprises Pvt Ltd.
Born in Chandernagore, India, Surajit has cross-functional experience in lifesciences, healthcare and management consulting; from managing P&L centres to starting up business divisions to strategy, operations and transactions. With a career spanning three continents, Surajit firmly believes in creating business with social impact. Along the way he has worked as an advisor to Government of India in various capacities, Multi Nationals to Indian companies and has converted to the cause of technology being the salvation of emerging markets in ensuring access and better treatment outcomes. Surajit is an avid reader and publisher of thought provoking content, insights and all the stuff in between.

Madhuri Banerjee
