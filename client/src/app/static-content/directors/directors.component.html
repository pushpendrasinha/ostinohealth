Girija Sankar Banerjee

Girija Sankar (GS), currently retired, has been instrumental in augmenting capabilities on the security side, under the aegis of Prime Minister’s Office - India, both at the domestic and international arena. Born in Nizam’s Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh, South India, in 1949 his career has taken him across Asia and Europe spanning more than three decades. Journeys tend to spur reflections and the more he has travelled across urban and rural areas, the more passionate he is about driving healthcare to help each person achieve his potential.

Madhuri Banerjee


Madhuri (M), is a counselor by trade, a technologist by profession with keen interest in leveraging software for healthcare solutions. Born in Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh, Central India, in 1984 her career has spanned Information Technology, student counseling as well as the technology education space. Technology is an enabler empowering people to lead healthy lives in sync with high productivity and working at the front lines of real world application of technology has ingrained in her a passion to leverage technology for a healthier world.
